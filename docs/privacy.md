# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-05

Thank you for using TT9 Smart. Your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## No Data Collection
The app **does not collect, store, or share any personal or typing-related data.** User privacy is fully respected, with no tracking, telemetry, or data reporting of any kind.

## No Spying, No Tracking
The app does not contain any analytics, advertising trackers, or background data collection mechanisms. Your usage remains entirely private.

## Permissions
- **Internet:** Required to download dictionaries. Network use is limited to downloading dictionaries and any required metadata; the app does not upload or share your personal, typing-related, or other user data.
- **Notifications:** Optionally needed to display dictionary loading progress, and when dictionary updates are available. Requested after first install or after an update on Android 13+. If you do not grant it, the app will download and update the dictionaries silently.
- **Microphone:** Required only for the optional Voice Input feature. Not needed by default, and will be requested only when you choose to use this feature. The app does not access or transmit any audio data without your explicit action.
- **Storage:** Required only when importing and exporting custom word lists. Not needed by default, and will be requested only when you choose to use this feature. The app does not access or transmit any files without your explicit action.

## Using Voice Input
Voice input relies on external speech recognition services, which may require an Internet connection. These services are not part of TT9 Smart and function independently.

When voice input is activated, your speech may be sent to third-party providers for processing. TT9 Smart does not record, transmit, or process the voice data, and cannot guarantee how external services handle your information.

If you are concerned about this, you may choose not to use the Voice Input feature and deny Microphone permissions when prompted.

## Open-Source Transparency
TT9 Smart is **open-source**, meaning that anyone can review the source code to verify the privacy claims above. You can inspect the code yourself to ensure that the app does not collect or transmit any data.

By using TT9 Smart, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app. This policy may be updated if necessary, and any changes will be reflected here.
